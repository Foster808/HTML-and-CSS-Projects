In 2019 the well known Crossfit Games changed drastically.

Leaving behind Regionals and introducing Sanctionals, Crossfit went from a six month season opening the year into a nine month season.

Crossift Games 2019 Champs
Mat Fraser (4x consecutive Fittest Male on Earth)


Tia Clair-Toomey (3x consecutive Fittest Female on Earth)

Both hold the current 2019 titles.
